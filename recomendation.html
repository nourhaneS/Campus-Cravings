New York University Area

Nestled on Saadiyat Island, the New York University Abu Dhabi area is home to a multicultural hub of
students from around the world. The area hosts a blend of cuisine styles to cater to its diverse community.
From cozy coffee shops perfect for study breaks, to a variety of eateries serving global flavors, this area
promises affordable options (thanks to all the discounts) without compromising on taste. Come discover
mouth-watering gems around NYUAD!
